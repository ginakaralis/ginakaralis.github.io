About me

My love for dancing began at the young age of 5 when I fell in love with a ballet dancer in a YouTube video.. and my love for it grew and grew and continues to grow every single day. As for my love for weddings, that began when I attended my cousin’s Jewish wedding when I was 12 years old. The way the groom looked at his bride walking down the aisle was for me not only the definition of love but it made me realise that 1) I am a hopeless romantic and 2) how weddings bring people together, even if it is for a brief afternoon EVERYONE is truly HAPPY!!

A long story short, I love to Dance and I love to teach people how to express themselves and have fun through dance. I do this for the pure love I have for dancing and because every person whom I teach gives me something in return. The energy and passion every couple has is different and by giving me the opportunity and challenge to create a bespoke choreography for each couple I find it both exhilarating and honouring. Your day is all about BOTH of you so your dance needs to portray that. I meet every couple beforehand and by getting to know them and their story I am able to create a choreography that represents THEM.
I love to see people happy and this is why I do what I do.
I teach all ages, whether it’s for a prom dance, wedding, special occasion or just wanting to learn how to dance.
