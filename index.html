Intro

Why True Rhythm I hear you ask??...True for the everlasting true love of a couple about to embark on one of the greatest journeys of their lives and Rhythm because let’s face it everyone has Rhythm….you just need help to find it!!
What kind of dancing you wonder??...Latin Dance which entails Rumba, Mambo, Salsa, Samba, Jive, Rock n’ Roll, Tango and Cha-cha-cha. All you have to do is choose your song which will dictate what kind of bespoke dance I will create with you.

About me

My love for dancing began at the age of 5 when I fell in love with an exotic ballet dancer in a movie…and my love for it grew and continues to grow every single day. As for my love for weddings, that began when I was 12 years old. No one can deny that even if it is for a brief afternoon EVERYONE is truly HAPPY!!

Long story short, I love to Dance and I love to teach people how to express themselves and have fun through dance. I do this for the pure love I have for dancing and because every person whom I teach gives me something in return as the energy and passion every couple has is different and by giving me the opportunity and challenge to create a bespoke choreography I am able to create a unique and mesmerizing dance.
